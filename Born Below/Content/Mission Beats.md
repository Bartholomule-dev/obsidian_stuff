# Mission Beats

Threshold-triggered narrative decision points during mission execution. Beats create the strategic depth of corruption schemes.

## Core Meter Beats

### Leverage Beats
Progress toward corruption success.

| Beat | Threshold | Choices | Key Tradeoff |
|------|-----------|---------|--------------|
| **Setup** | Low | Direct Approach / Patient Study | Speed vs Safety |
| **Milestone** | Medium | Accelerate / Consolidate | Push vs Secure |
| **Advantage** | High | Press Advantage / Savor Moment | Risk vs Stability |
| **Critical** | Max | Dramatic Finish / Quiet Finish / Delay | Glory vs Caution |

### Suspicion Beats
Managing detection risk.

| Beat | Threshold | Choices | Key Tradeoff |
|------|-----------|---------|--------------|
| **Setup** | Low | Erase Trace / Ignore It | Safety vs Progress |
| **Complication** | Medium | Push Through / Lay Low / Reframe | Aggression vs Retreat |
| **Defensive** | High | Strike Now / Withdraw | All-in vs Cut Losses |
| **Critical** | Max | Brazen Through / Tactical Retreat | Complete vs Abort |

### Moral Fatigue Beats
Breaking target's willpower.

| Beat | Threshold | Choices | Key Tradeoff |
|------|-----------|---------|--------------|
| **Setup** | Low | Push Compromise / Subtle Erosion | Fast vs Quiet |
| **Crack** | Medium | Exploit / Patient | Immediate vs Sustained |
| **Vulnerability** | High | False Comfort / Isolate | Manipulation vs Force |
| **Breaking Point** | Max | Break Them / Subtle Finish | Dramatic vs Clean |

## Beat Effects

All choices affect mission meters:

| Effect | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| `leverage +X` | Progress toward corruption |
| `suspicion +X` | Detection risk increase |
| `moral_fatigue +X` | Target willpower erosion |
| `stress +X` | Demon stress gain |
| `risk +X` | Intervention risk |
