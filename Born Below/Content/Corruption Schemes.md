# Corruption Schemes

Mission definitions for corrupting the three demo targets. Each scheme exploits a character's activities and vulnerabilities.

## The Knight

Focus: Honor, combat, loyalty, guilt

| Scheme | Description | Primary Sin |
|--------|-------------|-------------|
| **Tournament Training** | Exploit brutal regimen and flared temper | Wrath |
| **Guarding Queen** | Fan forbidden devotion and jealousy | Lust/Envy |
| **Investigating Threats** | Shape paranoia into obsessive surveillance | Pride |
| **Drowning Sorrows** | Use ale to wash away honor and oaths | Gluttony |
| **Seeking Confession** | Corrupt confessor to harvest secrets | Envy |
| **Proving Himself** | Offer impossible feats for glory | Pride |
| **Leading Men** | Amplify guilt over casualties | Wrath |

**Domain**: [[Explorations|Martial Quarter]]
**Discoveries**: Gambling debts, secret lovers, dishonorable past

---

## The Queen

Focus: Power, intrigue, ambition, control

| Scheme | Description | Primary Sin |
|--------|-------------|-------------|
| **Meeting Suitors** | Evaluate suitors as commodities | Greed |
| **Court Intrigue** | Navigate calculated smiles and debts | Pride |
| **Royal Celebration** | Use revelry to loosen tongues | Gluttony/Lust |
| **Securing Alliances** | Trade promises and threats | Greed |
| **Tightening Grip** | Consolidate power through crushed dissent | Wrath |
| **Private Grief** | Weaponize sorrow into calculating rage | Envy |
| **Secret Correspondence** | Guide treasonous responses | Pride |

**Domain**: [[Explorations|Royal Court]]
**Discoveries**: Affairs, political schemes, poisoners, illegitimate heirs

---

## The Bishop

Focus: Faith, doubt, power, hypocrisy

| Scheme | Description | Primary Sin |
|--------|-------------|-------------|
| **Rooting Out Heresy** | Turn purification into persecution | Wrath |
| **Leading Holy Rites** | Transform sermons into power displays | Pride |
| **Private Devotions** | Plant seeds of doubt during prayer | Sloth |
| **Crisis of Faith** | Guide toward apostasy | Envy |
| **Political Maneuvering** | Corrupt holy vows through politics | Greed |
| **Fasting & Penance** | Use delirium for false visions | Gluttony |
| **Hearing Confessions** | Use secrets as political leverage | Envy |

**Domain**: [[Explorations|Sacred District]]
**Discoveries**: Hidden wealth, crises of faith, forbidden knowledge

---

## Scheme Execution

Each scheme triggers [[Mission Beats]] at meter thresholds:
- **Leverage beats**: Progress toward corruption
- **Suspicion beats**: Risk management
- **Moral Fatigue beats**: Breaking willpower
