# Demo Targets

The three spotlight characters who must be corrupted to Stage 4 for demo victory.

## Victory Condition

**Win:** Corrupt the Knight, Bishop, AND Queen to Corruption Stage 4 (3+ needs at or below 10).

---

| **Personality** | Dutiful, Honor-bound, Methodical |

## Strategic Notes

### Difficulty Ranking
1. **Bishop** (Hardest) - 2.0 Holiness, highest divine protection
2. **Knight** (Medium) - 1.5 Holiness, clear weaknesses
3. **Queen** (Easiest) - 1.0 Holiness, political scheming lowers guard

### Recommended Order
Start with Queen (lowest resistance), then Knight, save Bishop for last when demons are stronger.
