# Verbs

Demon abilities used during corruption missions. Verbs are unlocked by reaching [[Seven Sins]] stat thresholds and equipped into loadouts before missions.

## Verb Categories

### Entry Verbs
Low-requirement abilities for early game.

| Verb | Stat Req | Effects |
|------|----------|---------|
| **Whisper** | Envy 30 | -10 Suspicion |
| **Corrupt Gift** | Greed 30 | +15 Leverage, +5 Suspicion |

### Intel Verbs
Information gathering for strategic advantage.

| Verb | Stat Req | Effects |
|------|----------|---------|
| **Detect Virtue** | Envy 30 | +5 Leverage, +8 Moral Fatigue |
| **Read Soul** | Pride 35 | Reveals all meter thresholds |

### Tempo Verbs
Sustained pressure on target's willpower.

| Verb | Stat Req | Effects |
|------|----------|---------|
| **Dream Invasion** | Sloth 35 | +15 Moral Fatigue, +5 Leverage |
| **Possess Servant** | Wrath 40 | +12 Moral Fatigue, +8 Leverage |
| **Gorge on Weakness** | Gluttony 35 | +18 Moral Fatigue, +5 Leverage |

### Control Verbs
Reduce suspicion and manage risk.

| Verb | Stat Req | Effects |
|------|----------|---------|
| **Honeyed Words** | Envy 35 | -12 Suspicion, -3 Intervention Risk |
| **Charm Offensive** | Lust 40 | -15 Suspicion |

### Spike Verbs
High-risk, high-reward burst abilities.

| Verb | Stat Req | Effects |
|------|----------|---------|
| **Infernal Bargain** | Greed 50 | +20 Leverage, +15 Stress, +8 Risk |
| **Intimidate** | Wrath 45 | +20 Leverage, +15 Suspicion, +5 Risk |

### Escape Verbs
Tactical retreat options.

| Verb | Stat Req | Effects |
|------|----------|---------|
| **Vanishing Act** | Lust 35 | Ends mission, -100 Suspicion, +3 Risk |
| **Shadow Step** | Sloth 40 | Ends mission, preserves 50% Leverage |

## Verb Progression

```
Foundation Training (+5 stat)
       ↓
Entry Verbs (30 req)
       ↓
Intel/Tempo/Control (35-40 req)
       ↓
Spike/Escape (45-50 req)
```

## Strategic Usage

| Situation | Recommended Verbs |
|-----------|-------------------|
| Early mission | Entry + Intel |
| Building pressure | Tempo verbs |
| High suspicion | Control verbs |
| Near completion | Spike verbs |
| Danger/abort | Escape verbs |
