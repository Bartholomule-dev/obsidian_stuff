# Weekly Activities

Rest and recovery options for demons not assigned to missions, training, or exploration.

## Available Activities

### Indulge Vices
Fast stress relief at high energy cost.

| Effect | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Stress | -40 |
| Energy | -40 |
| Best For | Emergency stress relief |

### Brood in Silence
Balanced recovery with mood penalty.

| Effect | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Energy | +20 |
| Stress | -20 |
| Happiness | -10 |
| Best For | Energy recovery |

### Torment Lesser Souls
Mood stabilization through cruelty.

| Effect | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Happiness | +15 |
| Stress | -15 |
| Energy | -20 |
| Best For | Happiness boost |

### Reflect on Memories
Strongest mood boost from nostalgia.

| Effect | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Happiness | +20 |
| Stress | -10 |
| Energy | -20 |
| Best For | Maximum happiness |

## Activity Selection

Demons not assigned to other activities can be set to weekly activities for:
- **Stress management** after intense missions
- **Energy recovery** between deployments
- **Happiness maintenance** for loyalty

## Personality Drift

Some activities can trigger [[Personality Evolution]] events:
- Excessive brooding → Melancholic drift
- Constant tormenting → Cruel drift
- Deep reflection → Thoughtful drift
