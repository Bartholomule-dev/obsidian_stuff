## Strategic Matchups

### Best for Knight (Wrath/Pride vulnerable)
- **Night Terror** - High Wrath for combat angles
- **Dark Sorceress** - Pride focus

### Best for Queen (Pride/Envy/Lust vulnerable)
- **Jealous Watcher** - Highest Envy
- **Serpent Seductress** - High Lust
- **Shapeshifting Deceiver** - Envy + deception

### Best for Bishop (Greed/Pride/Gluttony vulnerable)
- **Ravenous Vine** - High Gluttony
- **Blood Drainer** - Gluttony specialist
- **Scavenger Rat** - Greed focus
