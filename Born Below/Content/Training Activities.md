# Training Activities

Multi-week stat improvement activities for demons. Training unlocks [[Verbs]] by raising [[Seven Sins]] stats.

## Foundation Training (+5 Single Stat)

One week duration, focused stat growth.

| Activity | Stat | Cost | Description |
|----------|------|------|-------------|
| **Wrath Drills** | Wrath +5 | 40 SE | Combat fury cultivation |
| **Greed Exercises** | Greed +5 | 40 SE | Avarice amplification |
| **Pride Conditioning** | Pride +5 | 40 SE | Ego inflation training |
| **Envy Practice** | Envy +5 | 40 SE | Jealousy development |
| **Lust Temptation** | Lust +5 | 40 SE | Desire enhancement |
| **Gluttony Indulgence** | Gluttony +5 | 40 SE | Excess cultivation |
| **Sloth Meditation** | Sloth +5 | 40 SE | Apathy training |

## Dual-Sin Training (+3/+3 Two Stats)

Advanced training combining two sins.

| Activity | Stats | Cost | Requirements |
|----------|-------|------|--------------|
| **Wrath & Gluttony Rampage** | Wrath +3, Gluttony +3 | 100 SE | 50+ total corruption |
| **Envy & Lust Manipulation** | Envy +3, Lust +3 | 100 SE | 50+ total corruption |
| **Pride & Greed Aristocrat** | Pride +3, Greed +3 | 100 SE | 50+ total corruption |
| **Sloth & Envy Despair** | Sloth +3, Envy +3 | 100 SE | 50+ total corruption |
| **Wrath & Pride Tactics** | Wrath +3, Pride +3 | 100 SE | 50+ total corruption |
| **Envy & Greed Schemes** | Envy +3, Greed +3 | 100 SE | 50+ total corruption |
| **Lust & Pride Seduction** | Lust +3, Pride +3 | 100 SE | 50+ total corruption |

## Training Management

- Training locks demon for duration (1 week)
- Cannot be assigned to missions during training
- Completion triggers [[DemonMilestoneTemplate]] event
- Managed by [[DemonTrainingManager]]
